Crafting Timepieces Since 1995

Founded by master watchmaker Elias Thorne, ZenTime has been creating exceptional timepieces for over two decades. Our philosophy is simple: combine traditional craftsmanship with innovative design to create watches that are both beautiful and reliable.

Each ZenTime watch is assembled by hand in our Swiss workshop, with every component meticulously inspected to ensure it meets our exacting standards. We believe that a watch should be more than just a timekeeping device—it should be a reflection of personal style and a companion for life's journeys.
